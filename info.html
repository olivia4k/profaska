Justyna Profaska is a an architect and a PhD Fellow in Landscape and Environment at La Sapienza University of Rome. Her research focuses on the perception of the space and sense of the community in the digital world. Through the investigation of simulacrum of landscapes in Virtual Reality, her goal is to understand the impact of new media on the society, and to more about the meaning of places and its narrative in the current day hyperreality.
